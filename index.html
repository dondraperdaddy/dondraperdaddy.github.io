Background

As a Ph.D. Candidate in Comparative Literature at the University of Western Ontario, I operate as a high-fidelity data engine bridging extreme literary precision with AI engineering and media production. Fluent in English, Mandarin, French, and Italian, I specialize in semantic evaluation, cross-cultural alignment, and end-to-end content strategy.

Technical & Creative Workflows

AI Evaluation & Alignment: Implementing LLM Benchmarking, Hallucination Mitigation, Creative Prose Evaluation, and RLHF protocols. I codify subjective aesthetic values—style, tone, and voice—into actionable, machine-readable rubrics.

Multimodal Pipelines: Executing generative AI workflows (Midjourney), advanced speech synthesis (ElevenLabs, Whisper ASR), and workflow automation via Python scripting to push the boundaries of machine-generated content.

Content Strategy: Directing end-to-end production for digital platforms. I manage algorithm optimization, creator relations, and full-stack media generation, leveraging tools such as Descript and Riverside.fm.
